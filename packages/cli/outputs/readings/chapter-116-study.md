### The Danger of Giving Place

Paul issues a specific warning:

> _"Be ye angry, and sin not: let not the sun go down upon your wrath: neither give place to the devil."_ (Ephesians 4:26, 27)

The phrase "give place" (Greek: _topos_) means to yield ground, to allow a foothold. Cherished anger becomes a landing zone for demonic influence. What begins as human emotion, if nursed and harbored, becomes a stronghold for the enemy.

## Principles and Great Controversy Themes

### The Sanctuary and Spiritual Warfare

The sanctuary reveals the weapons God has provided for this cosmic battle:

- **The altar of sacrifice** (outer court): The blood of Christ is our defense. "They overcame him by the blood of the Lamb" (Revelation 12:11). Satan cannot stand against the merits of Christ's sacrifice applied by faith.

- **The table of showbread** (holy place): God's Word is our sword. Jesus defeated Satan's temptations with "It is written" (Matthew 4:4, 7, 10). Daily feeding on Scripture builds spiritual strength.

- **The altar of incense** (holy place): Prayer is our communication with heaven's command center. "Praying always with all prayer and supplication in the Spirit" (Ephesians 6:18).

- **The Most Holy Place**: Christ our High Priest intercedes for us, and in the investigative judgment, Satan's accusations against God's people are answered by the righteousness of Christ.

## Practical Application

### How to Stand Victorious Today
