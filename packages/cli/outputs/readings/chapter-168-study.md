## Principles and Great Controversy Themes

### The Family as a Reflection of Divine Government

The home is meant to be a miniature heaven on earthâ€”a place where God's principles of love, order, and authority are experienced in daily life. Satan knows this, which is why he targets the family with relentless fury.

**Children who learn to honor earthly parents are being prepared to honor their heavenly Father.** Conversely, children who despise parental authority often struggle to submit to God.

## Practical Application

**For Parents:**

1. **Begin with your own heart.** Are God's words living in you? You cannot impart what you do not possess.
2. **Make instruction intentional and integrated.** Do not relegate spiritual teaching to Sabbath onlyâ€”weave it into every day.
3. **Correct early, consistently, and lovingly.** Do not discipline in anger. Let correction be followed by explanation and reconciliation.
4. **Guard against harshness.** Your goal is training, not breaking. A discouraged child is not a trained child.
5. **Pray daily for each child by name.** Only God can convert the heartâ€”your role is to cooperate with His work.

**For Children (of any age):**

1. **Honor is lifelong.** Even adult children are called to respect and care for aging parents (Proverbs 23:22).
2. **Obedience is worship.** When you obey your parents in the Lord, you please God Himself.
3. **Forgive imperfect parents.** No earthly parent is perfect. Honor the position even when the person fails.
4. **Learn submission to authority.** The home is training ground for your relationship with God. A heart that rebels against parents will struggle to yield to God.

**For the Elderly:**

> _"Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I am the LORD."_ (Leviticus 19:32)

Age itself commands respect. A society that discards its elderly has lost reverence for God Himself.
