## Principles and Great Controversy Themes

### The Central Battlefield

The birth of Christ reveals that this world is the theater of a cosmic conflict. Satan sought to destroy the Messiah before His mission could be accomplished. Every prophecy that guided the wise men, every angelic warning that preserved the holy family, demonstrates Heaven's active involvement in this warfare.
