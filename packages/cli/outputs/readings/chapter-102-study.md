## Deeper Truths

### The Mystery of Iniquity

How did sin originate in a perfect being created by a perfect God? Scripture gives us glimpses but preserves a certain mystery:

> _"Thou wast perfect in thy ways from the day that thou wast created, till iniquity was found in thee."_ (Ezekiel 28:15)

The text says iniquity was "found" in himâ€”it appeared where before there was none. God did not create evil; He created a free being who chose to rebel.

The word "merchandise" suggests traffickingâ€”a spreading of disaffection, a trading in falsehood. Satan circulated his discontent among the angels until rebellion ripened into open war.

The cross demonstrates how far God was willing to go to redeem the results of that freedom. Moreover, the universe will eventually see that God's way is the only way that leads to happiness, and rebellion will never arise again (Nahum 1:9).

## Principles and Great Controversy Themes

### The Cosmic Conflict

The great controversy between Christ and Satan forms the backdrop of all human history. Satan's initial accusations in heaven questioned God's character, His law, and His government:

- Is God truly loving, or is He arbitrary and severe?
- Is God's law necessary, or is it an unnecessary burden?
- Can created beings truly keep God's law, or is obedience impossible?

The plan of salvation answers each accusation:

- **The cross** reveals God's infinite love (John 3:16; Romans 5:8)
- **The law** is shown to be holy, just, and goodâ€”the transcript of God's character (Romans 7:12; Psalm 19:7)
- **Christ's life and the lives of His faithful followers** demonstrate that obedience is possible through divine grace (Philippians 4:13; Revelation 14:12)

### Satan's War Against the Church

> _"And when the dragon saw that he was cast unto the earth, he persecuted the woman which brought forth the man child."_ (Revelation 12:13)

The "woman" represents God's church throughout history. Satan, unable to destroy Christ directly, has turned his rage upon Christ's followers. The blood of countless martyrs testifies to this ongoing war.

> _"And the dragon was wroth with the woman, and went to make war with the remnant of her seed, which keep the commandments of God, and have the testimony of Jesus Christ."_ (Revelation 12:17)

The "remnant"â€”God's last-day peopleâ€”will face Satan's final fury. Why? ### Satan's End-Time Deceptions

As the end approaches, Satan's deceptions intensify:

> _"And deceiveth them that dwell on the earth by the means of those miracles which he had power to do."_ (Revelation 13:14)

> _"For they are the spirits of devils, working miracles, which go forth unto the kings of the earth and of the whole world, to gather them to the battle of that great day of God Almighty."_ (Revelation 16:14)

Miracles alone are no proof of truth! Satan can counterfeit supernatural signs. Only faithfulness to God's Word provides safety.

After the millennium, Satan will make one last attempt at conquest:
