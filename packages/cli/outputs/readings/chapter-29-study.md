## Deeper Truths

### Scripture's Primary Purpose: Doctrine

> _"All Scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness."_ (2 Timothy 3:16)

Notice that _doctrine_ is mentioned first among Scripture's profitable uses. Before we can be reproved, corrected, or instructed in righteous living, we must first understand what is true.
