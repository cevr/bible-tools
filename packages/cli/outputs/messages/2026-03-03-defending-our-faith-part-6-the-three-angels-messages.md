## Section 1 — The First Angel: The Everlasting Gospel and the Judgment Hour (10 min)

_Addresses: "This is just SDA invention," "Revelation is too symbolic"_

### A. The Everlasting Gospel — Not a New Gospel

- Revelation 14:6 — "The everlasting gospel"
  - _Euangelion aionion_ = eternal, age-abiding good news
  - [WB]: Write "EVERLASTING GOSPEL — same gospel, final context" (Rev 14:6)
  - Not a replacement of the gospel of Christ — it _is_ the gospel of Christ,
    proclaimed in its final, fullest setting
- The gospel has always included:
  - Creation (God as Creator — Gen 1)
  - Redemption (the cross — John 3:16)
  - Judgment (accountability — Eccl 12:14)
  - Restoration (new earth — Rev 21)
- The first angel emphasizes what the modern church has neglected: _judgment and
  creation_

### B. "Fear God and Give Glory to Him"

- "Fear God" — _phobethete ton theon_ = reverence, awe, take seriously
  - Not terror — covenant loyalty
  - Ecclesiastes 12:13 — "Fear God, and keep his commandments: for this is the
    whole duty of man"
  - [WB]: Write "FEAR GOD = reverence + obedience" (Eccl 12:13)
- "Give glory to him" — glory = character
  - 1 Corinthians 10:31 — "Whether therefore ye eat, or drink, or whatsoever ye
    do, do all to the glory of God"
  - This touches everything — diet, conduct, worship, thought
  - [RQ]: If glorifying God involves how we eat and drink, does the health
    message belong to the gospel? (1 Cor 10:31)

### C. "The Hour of His Judgment Is Come"

- Present tense: the judgment _is_ here — not "will come" but "is come"
  - This is the investigative judgment we studied in Part 5
  - The first angel announces it to the world
- "Worship him that made heaven, and earth, and the sea, and the fountains of
  waters"
  - [WB]: Write side by side: REV 14:7 / EXOD 20:11
  - Revelation 14:7: "made heaven, and earth, and the sea"
  - Exodus 20:11: "made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is"
  - The first angel's message is a direct call back to the fourth commandment
  - [RQ]: When the first angel uses creation language identical to the Sabbath
    commandment, is the Sabbath incidental or central to this message? (Rev
    14:7; Exod 20:11)
- [EGW]: "The message of Revelation 14, proclaiming that the hour of God's
  judgment is come, is given in the time of the end; and the angel of Revelation
  10 is represented as having one foot on the sea and one foot on the land,
  showing that the message will be carried to distant lands." (DA 234.1)

## Section 2 — The Second Angel: Babylon Is Fallen (8 min)

_Addresses: "Babylon is ancient history," "You're just attacking other
churches"_

### A. What Is Babylon?

- Revelation 14:8 — "Babylon is fallen, is fallen, that great city"
- Babylon in Scripture represents _confusion_ and _false worship_:
  - Genesis 11:9 — _Babel_ = confusion — humanity's first organized rebellion
    against God
  - Daniel 1–4 — Babylon as a system that demands false worship (Dan 3 — the
    golden image)
  - [WB]: Write "BABYLON = confusion + false worship" (Gen 11:9; Dan 3)
- In Revelation, Babylon is not a single city or nation — it is a global
  religious system that opposes God's truth
  - Revelation 17:5 — "MYSTERY, BABYLON THE GREAT, THE MOTHER OF HARLOTS AND
    ABOMINATIONS OF THE EARTH"
  - A _mother_ implies _daughters_ — a family of error, not a single entity

### B. What Has Babylon Fallen From?

- "Is fallen" = has collapsed spiritually — moral and doctrinal corruption
- Babylon's fall involves embracing errors that contradict Scripture:
  - Immortality of the soul (from Greek philosophy, not the Bible)
  - Sunday worship (from Roman decree, not apostolic command)
  - Eternal hellfire (from medieval theology, not biblical teaching)
  - Salvation without transformation (cheap grace)
  - [WB]: List the four errors in a column under "BABYLON'S WINE"
- Revelation 14:8 — "She made all nations drink of the wine of the wrath of her
  fornication"
  - Wine = intoxicating false doctrines
  - Fornication = illicit union of church and state
  - [RQ]: If Babylon's wine is false doctrine accepted by "all nations," how
    widespread is the deception? (Rev 14:8)

### C. Not an Attack — A Rescue Call

- Revelation 18:4 — "Come out of her, my people"
  - God has people _in_ Babylon — sincere believers trapped in error
  - The second angel's message is not condemnation of individuals — it is
    exposure of a _system_
  - [WB]: Write "GOD'S PEOPLE IN BABYLON → CALLED OUT" (Rev 18:4)
- [RQ]: If God calls them "my people," does He love them? Then why call them
  out? Because love warns. (Rev 18:4)
- [EGW]: "Babylon is said to be 'the mother of harlots.' By her daughters must
  be symbolized churches that cling to her doctrines and traditions." (GC 382.3)

## Section 3 — The Third Angel: The Mark and the Seal (8 min) [*]

_Addresses: "You're fear-mongering," "The mark is a chip/barcode"_

### A. The Third Angel's Warning

- Revelation 14:9–11 — The most solemn warning in all Scripture
  - "If any man worship the beast and his image, and receive his mark in his
    forehead, or in his hand, the same shall drink of the wine of the wrath of
    God"
  - This is not metaphorical wrath — it is the seven last plagues (Rev 15–16)
  - [WB]: Write "WORSHIP BEAST + RECEIVE MARK → WRATH OF GOD" (Rev 14:9–10)
- The warning is severe because the choice is final — it marks the close of
  probation

### B. The Mark Is About Worship, Not Technology

- The mark is not a microchip, barcode, or vaccine
- It is received in the _forehead_ (belief/conviction) or the _hand_
  (action/compliance)
  - [WB]: Write "FOREHEAD = conviction" / "HAND = compliance" (Rev 14:9)
- The issue in Revelation 13–14 is consistently _worship_:
  - Revelation 13:4 — "They worshipped the beast"
  - Revelation 13:8 — "All that dwell upon the earth shall worship him"
  - Revelation 13:12 — "Causeth the earth... to worship the first beast"
  - Revelation 13:15 — "Cause that as many as would not worship the image of the
    beast should be killed"
- [RQ]: When every reference to the beast's mark involves _worship_, should we
  look for its meaning in worship practices or in technology? (Rev 13:4, 8, 12, 15)

### C. Seal vs. Mark — A Contrast of Allegiance

- The seal of God is placed in the forehead (Rev 7:3; 14:1)
  - In Part 2 we established: the Sabbath is God's seal — containing His name,
    title, and territory (Exod 20:8–11)
- The mark of the beast is the counterfeit — a rival claim to authority over
  worship
  - Daniel 7:25 — The power that would "think to change times and laws"
  - The change of the Sabbath to Sunday is Rome's claimed mark of authority
  - [WB]: Write "SEAL (Sabbath — God's authority)" vs. "MARK (Sunday — man's
    authority)" (Rev 7:3; Rev 13:16; Dan 7:25)
- The mark becomes the mark of the beast only when:
  1. The truth about the Sabbath is clearly understood
  2. Sunday worship is enforced by law
  3. Individuals choose human authority over God's command _with full knowledge_
- [EB]: No one has the mark of the beast now — it becomes an issue when
  enforcement comes. Millions of Sunday-keepers are sincere believers whom God
  loves (Rev 18:4).
- [EGW]: "When Sunday observance shall be enforced by law, and the world shall
  be enlightened concerning the obligation of the true Sabbath, then whoever
  shall transgress the command of God, to obey a precept which has no higher
  authority than that of Rome, will thereby honor popery above God." (GC 449.2)

## FINAL PEAK SECTION — God's Final Generation (5 min)

### A. The Three Messages Are One Message

- They are not three separate sermons — they are one unified proclamation:
  1. Worship the Creator (return to true worship, including Sabbath)
  2. Reject Babylon's errors (come out of false systems)
  3. Refuse the mark (choose God's authority over man's)
  - [WB]: Write 'ONE MESSAGE: Worship God → Leave Babylon → Refuse the Mark →
Keep the Commandments + Faith of Jesus'
- Revelation 14:12 — "Here is the patience of the saints: here are they that
  keep the commandments of God, and the faith of Jesus"
  - This is the result — a people shaped by these messages
  - Commandments _and_ faith — law _and_ gospel — never one without the other

### B. This Is Not Fear-Mongering — It Is the Final Invitation

- The three angels' messages are introduced as "the everlasting gospel" (Rev
  14:6) — good news
- They are followed by the harvest (Rev 14:14–16) — Christ's return
- Between the messages and the harvest is the character of the saints (v. 12) —
  this is what God is producing
- [RQ]: If the three angels' messages produce people who have both the
  commandments of God and the faith of Jesus, is this legalism or the fullest
  expression of the gospel? (Rev 14:12)

### C. You Are Called to Proclaim This

- Revelation 14:6 — "To every nation, and kindred, and tongue, and people"
  - This message has a global reach — it is not denominational trivia
- God needs a people who will carry this message to the world
  - Not with arrogance but with urgency and love

### Call to Action — Carry the Message

- **Receive** the everlasting gospel in its fullness — creation, redemption,
  judgment, and restoration (Rev 14:6–7)
- **Separate** from Babylon's errors — test every belief by Scripture, not by
  tradition (Isa 8:20; Rev 18:4)
- **Choose** God's seal over any human substitute — the Sabbath is the sign of
  your allegiance to the Creator (Rev 7:3; Exod 20:8–11)
- **Proclaim** these messages with urgency and compassion — God has people in
  Babylon who need to hear the call (Rev 18:4)
- **Resolve**: _I will stand with the remnant who keep the commandments of God
  and the faith of Jesus, whatever the cost_ (Rev 14:12)
