## Section 1 — Satan's Attack: The Bridge Destroyed (7 min)

### The Strategy of Deception

- Genesis 3:1 — Serpent questions God's word: "Yea, hath God said?"
  - Attack on divine authority and trustworthiness
  - [WB]: Write — "ATTACK 1: Doubt God's Word" (Gen 3:1)
- Genesis 3:4-5 — Direct contradiction: "Ye shall not surely die"
  - Attack on divine truthfulness
  - Promise of godhood without God
  - [WB]: Add — "ATTACK 2: Deny Consequences" (Gen 3:4)

### The Fall — Witnesses Compromised

- Genesis 3:6 — Eve saw, took, ate, gave to Adam
  - Senses overrode revelation
  - Trust transferred from God to self
- [RQ]: In what way did humanity's fall damage their role as witnesses for God?
  (Gen 3:6-7)
- [EGW]: "Satan represented to the holy pair that they would be gainers by
  breaking the law of God... This is the same temptation which Satan brought to
  Christ, and which he is continually presenting to man." (PP 53.2)
- [EB]: Isaiah 14:12-14 — Satan's original lie: ascend, be like the Most High

### The Immediate Consequence

- Genesis 3:7 — Eyes opened, knew nakedness, made coverings
  - Self-awareness replaced God-awareness
  - Human works attempted to cover sin
- [Aside]: The first religion invented by man: fig leaves. Human effort to solve
  a divine problem.

## Section 2 — The Devastating Result: Separation (6 min) [*]

### The Broken Connection

- Genesis 3:8 — Hid themselves from the presence of the LORD
  - Fellowship replaced by fear
  - [WB]: Modify diagram — HEAVEN ✕ EARTH (broken line, X in middle) (Gen 3:8)
- Genesis 3:23-24 — Driven out from Eden, cherubim guard the way
  - Access to tree of life blocked
  - Physical expulsion mirrors spiritual separation
- [RQ]: What does hiding from God reveal about sin's effect on the human heart?
  (Gen 3:8)

### The Scope of the Devastation

- Isaiah 59:2 — "Your iniquities have separated between you and your God"
  - Sin is not merely broken rules — broken relationship
- Romans 6:23 — "The wages of sin is death"
  - Not arbitrary punishment — natural consequence of disconnection from Life
- [WB]: Under broken line, write — "SEPARATION = DEATH" (Rom 6:23)
- [EGW]: "In consequence of sin, communion between heaven and earth was broken."
  (DA 115.2)
- [EB]: Ezekiel 18:4 — The soul that sinneth, it shall die
- [EB]: Ephesians 2:12 — Without Christ: aliens, strangers, no hope, without God

### The Cosmic Damage

- Witnesses corrupted — Satan claims victory
- Earth now enemy territory — Satan "prince of this world" (John 12:31)
- Universe watches in horror
- [RQ]: With the bridge between heaven and earth destroyed, what hope remained
  for humanity or for God's cause? (Gen 3:24)

## Section 3 — God's Immediate Remedy: The Sanctuary Bridge (7 min)

### The First Gospel — God Moves First

- Genesis 3:9 — "Where art thou?" — God seeks the lost
  - Not accusation but invitation
  - Grace precedes judgment
- Genesis 3:15 — The protoevangelium
  - Seed of woman to crush serpent's head
  - Promise of a coming Deliverer
  - [WB]: Under broken line, draw arrow pointing to — "PROMISE: Gen 3:15" (Gen
    3:15)
- Genesis 3:21 — LORD God made coats of skins
  - First death — first sacrifice — first covering by blood
  - Substitution introduced
- [EGW]: "The instant man accepted the temptations of Satan... a Saviour was
  provided." (RH, March 12, 1901)

### Jacob's Ladder — The Bridge Restored in Vision

- Genesis 28:12 — Ladder set on earth, top reaching heaven
  - Angels ascending and descending
  - Heaven and earth reconnected
  - [WB]: Replace broken line with LADDER — base on EARTH, top in HEAVEN (Gen
    28:12)
- Genesis 28:13 — LORD stood above it
  - God initiates restoration
  - Covenant promise renewed
- [RQ]: What did Jacob understand that ladder to represent? (Gen 28:16-17)
- Genesis 28:17 — "This is none other but the house of God... the gate of
  heaven"
  - Jacob names it Bethel — "House of God"
  - The sanctuary concept revealed

### Christ Declares Himself the Ladder

- John 1:51 — "Ye shall see heaven open, and the angels of God ascending and
  descending upon the Son of man"
  - Christ is the ladder
  - Christ is the bridge
  - [WB]: Write on the ladder — "CHRIST" (John 1:51)
- [EGW]: "The ladder represents Jesus, the appointed medium of communication.
  Had He not with His own merits bridged the gulf that sin had made, the
  ministering angels could have held no communion with fallen man." (PP 184.2)

### The Sanctuary Pattern — God's Dwelling Place

- Exodus 25:8 — "Let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them"
  - God's solution to separation: a meeting place
  - [WB]: At base of ladder, write — "SANCTUARY = Meeting Place" (Ex 25:8)
- Exodus 25:9 — "According to all that I shew thee, after the pattern"
  - Earthly copy of heavenly reality
  - God reveals the blueprint
- Hebrews 8:5 — Moses served unto "the example and shadow of heavenly things"
  - Earthly sanctuary points upward
- [EB]: Exodus 25:40 — Make all things according to the pattern shown in the
  mount

### The Sanctuary Typifies Christ

- The Lamb — John 1:29 — "Behold the Lamb of God"
  - [WB]: List on side — "LAMB = Christ's sacrifice" (John 1:29)
- The Bread — John 6:35 — "I am the bread of life"
  - [WB]: Add — "BREAD = Christ's sustenance" (John 6:35)
- The Light — John 8:12 — "I am the light of the world"
  - [WB]: Add — "LIGHT = Christ's guidance" (John 8:12)
- The Incense — Hebrews 7:25 — "Ever liveth to make intercession"
  - [WB]: Add — "INCENSE = Christ's intercession" (Heb 7:25)
- The High Priest — Hebrews 8:1-2 — Minister of the true tabernacle
  - [WB]: Add — "HIGH PRIEST = Christ Himself" (Heb 8:1-2)
- [RQ]: Why did every element of the sanctuary point to Christ? (Col 2:17)
- [Aside]: Walk through the sanctuary and you walk through Christ's work — His
  death, His life, His intercession, His presence.

## FINAL PEAK SECTION — The Bridge Stands Open Now (5 min)

### The True Tabernacle Revealed

- Hebrews 9:11-12 — Christ entered the greater and more perfect tabernacle
  - Not made with hands
  - By His own blood
  - Obtained eternal redemption
- [WB]: Complete diagram — Ladder with CHRIST, reaching from EARTH (sanctuary)
  to HEAVEN (throne of God) (Heb 9:11-12)

### The Way is Open — Will You Walk It?

- Hebrews 10:19-20 — "Boldness to enter... by a new and living way"
  - The veil = His flesh
  - Access granted through Christ's sacrifice
- Hebrews 4:16 — "Come boldly unto the throne of grace"
  - The bridge is not barricaded — it is beckoning
- [RQ]: If the bridge between heaven and earth stands restored in Christ, why do
  many still live as though separated from God? (Heb 10:19)
- [EGW]: "Christ is the connecting link between God and man. He has promised His
  personal intercession. He places the whole virtue of His righteousness on the
  side of the suppliant." (COL 148.1)

### The Urgency of the Open Door

- Hebrews 9:24 — Christ "now to appear in the presence of God for us"
  - Present ministry — present opportunity
- Revelation 3:7-8 — He opens and no man shuts
  - But doors do not remain open forever
- [EB]: John 14:6 — "I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto
  the Father, but by me"

### Call to Action — Cross the Bridge

- The sanctuary stands as God's invitation to return
- Christ is the ladder — the only way back to the Father (John 14:6)
- The bridge is open **now** — His intercession continues **now** (Heb 9:24)
- **Enter through Christ today:**
  - Accept His sacrifice as your only covering (Gen 3:21)
  - Surrender as a witness restored to God's service (1 Cor 4:9)
  - Walk the sanctuary way — dying to self at the altar, feeding on Christ as
    Bread, following His Light, depending on His intercession
  - Come boldly to the throne of grace while access remains (Heb 4:16)
- The connection Satan severed, Christ has rebuilt — **will you cross?**
