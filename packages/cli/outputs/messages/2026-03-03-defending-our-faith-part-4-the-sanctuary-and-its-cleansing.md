## Section 1 — The Heavenly Sanctuary Is Real (8 min)

_Addresses: "There is no literal sanctuary in heaven"_

### A. The Earthly Was a Copy of the Heavenly

- Exodus 25:8–9 — "Let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them.
  According to all that I shew thee, after the pattern"
  - _Tabnith_ (#8403) = pattern, model, blueprint
  - [WB]: Write "EARTHLY = COPY" → "HEAVENLY = ORIGINAL" (Exod 25:9)
- Exodus 25:40 — "Look that thou make them after their pattern, which was shewed
  thee in the mount"
  - Moses saw the heavenly original on Sinai
- Hebrews 8:5 — The priests "serve unto the example and shadow of heavenly
  things"
  - _Hupodeigma_ (#5262) = copy, example
  - _Skia_ (#4639) = shadow
  - The earthly services were a shadow — the reality is in heaven

### B. The New Testament Confirms the Heavenly Sanctuary

- Hebrews 8:1–2 — Christ is "a minister of the sanctuary, and of the true
  tabernacle, which the Lord pitched, and not man"
  - _Alethinos_ (#228) = true, genuine, real — as opposed to the copy
  - [RQ]: If the heavenly sanctuary is merely a metaphor, what does Christ
    minister in? (Heb 8:2)
- Hebrews 9:11 — Christ entered "a greater and more perfect tabernacle, not made
  with hands"
- Hebrews 9:24 — "Christ is not entered into the holy places made with hands,
  which are the figures of the true; but into heaven itself"
  - [WB]: Write "MADE WITH HANDS = earthly (figure)" vs. "NOT MADE WITH HANDS =
    heavenly (true)" (Heb 9:24)

### C. Revelation Shows the Sanctuary Furniture in Heaven

- Revelation 1:12 — Seven golden candlesticks (lampstand — Holy Place furniture)
- Revelation 8:3 — Golden altar of incense before the throne
- Revelation 11:19 — "The temple of God was opened in heaven, and there was seen
  in his temple the ark of his testament"
  - [WB]: Write 'REVELATION SHOWS: Candlestick (1:12) + Altar of Incense (8:3)
+ Ark of the Covenant (11:19)'
  - The ark contains the Ten Commandments — the law is in heaven, in the Most
    Holy Place
  - [RQ]: If the ark of the covenant is seen in God's heavenly temple in
    Revelation 11:19, can we dismiss the heavenly sanctuary as figurative? (Rev
    11:19)
- [EGW]: "The sanctuary in heaven, in which Jesus ministers in our behalf, is the
  great original, of which the sanctuary built by Moses was a copy." (GC 414.1)

## Section 2 — Two Phases of Ministry (8 min)

_Addresses: "Jesus sat down — His work is done," "No need for further
priestly ministry"_

### A. The Daily Ministry — Holy Place

- In the earthly sanctuary, the daily service involved:
  - Morning and evening sacrifice (Exod 29:38–42)
  - Incense burned on the golden altar (Exod 30:7–8)
  - Blood sprinkled — sin transferred from sinner to sanctuary
  - [WB]: Draw sanctuary layout: COURT (altar) → HOLY PLACE (table, lampstand,
    incense altar) → MOST HOLY PLACE (ark)
- This represented Christ's continuous intercession for His people
  - Hebrews 7:25 — "He ever liveth to make intercession for them"
  - 1 John 2:1 — "If any man sin, we have an advocate with the Father"
- Christ "sat down" at the right hand of God (Heb 1:3) — but sitting indicates
  royal authority, not cessation of priestly work
  - Hebrews 8:1–2 explicitly says He is _ministering_ in the sanctuary now

### B. The Yearly Ministry — Most Holy Place (Day of Atonement)

- Leviticus 16 — Once a year, the high priest entered the Most Holy Place
  - Leviticus 16:16 — "He shall make an atonement for the holy place, because of
    the uncleanness of the children of Israel"
  - The sanctuary itself was cleansed of accumulated sin
  - [WB]: Write "DAILY: Sin transferred TO sanctuary" → "YEARLY: Sin purged
    FROM sanctuary" (Lev 16:16)
- Leviticus 16:30 — "On that day shall the priest make an atonement for you, to
  cleanse you, that ye may be clean from all your sins before the LORD"
  - Dual cleansing: sanctuary cleansed _and_ people cleansed
- This yearly service = the Day of Atonement = _Yom Kippur_ = a day of judgment
  - Leviticus 23:29 — "Whatsoever soul it be that shall not be afflicted in that
    same day, he shall be cut off from among his people"
  - [RQ]: If the Day of Atonement involved judgment and separation, what does
    its heavenly antitype involve? (Lev 23:29)

### C. The Earthly Pattern Points to Two Phases of Heavenly Ministry

- Phase 1: Christ's daily intercession in the Holy Place — from His ascension
  onward
- Phase 2: Christ's special ministry in the Most Holy Place — the antitypical Day
  of Atonement
  - [WB]: Write "PHASE 1: Holy Place (AD 31 → 1844)" → "PHASE 2: Most Holy
    Place (1844 → Second Coming)"
- Hebrews 9:23 — "It was therefore necessary that the patterns of things in the
  heavens should be purified with these; but the heavenly things themselves with
  better sacrifices than these"
  - The heavenly sanctuary requires cleansing — Hebrews says so explicitly
  - [RQ]: If the heavenly things need purifying (Heb 9:23), what is Daniel
    8:14's cleansing if not this very event?

## Section 3 — The 2300 Days and 1844 (10 min)

_Addresses: "1844 was a cover-up," "Nothing happened in 1844"_

### A. The Timeline Reviewed

- Daniel 8:14 — "Unto two thousand and three hundred days; then shall the
  sanctuary be cleansed"
  - _Tsadaq_ (#6663) = to be made right, vindicated, cleansed, justified
  - LXX uses _katharisthesetai_ = shall be cleansed (same word family as Heb
    9:23)
- Daniel 9:24 — The 70 weeks are "cut off" (_chathak_) from a larger period
  - The only larger time period in Daniel 8 is the 2300 days
  - Same starting point: 457 BC (Artaxerxes' decree)
  - [WB]: Timeline: 457 BC → (490 years / 70 weeks) → AD 34 ... → (2300 years
    total) → **1844 AD**
- The 70 weeks are the key that unlocks the 2300 days
  - Part 3 proved the 70 weeks are years — Christ's baptism (AD 27) and
    crucifixion (AD 31) confirm it
  - The same principle applied to the full 2300 gives us 1844

### B. What Happened in 1844 — Not Earth, but Heaven

- The Millerites expected Christ's return in 1844 — they were wrong about the
  _event_ but right about the _date_
  - [WB]: Write 'MILLERITES: Right date, wrong event'
- Daniel 8:14 does not say "then shall the earth be cleansed" — it says "the
  sanctuary"
- The sanctuary to be cleansed is the heavenly sanctuary — where Christ
  ministers (Heb 8:1–2)
- In 1844, Christ moved from the Holy Place to the Most Holy Place — the
  antitypical Day of Atonement began
  - [WB]: Write '1844: Christ enters Most Holy Place → Investigative Judgment
begins'
- [RQ]: If the Millerites had the right date by the day-year principle (proven
  by the 70 weeks), is it more reasonable to say the date was wrong or to
  re-examine the event? (Dan 8:14)

### C. This Was Not a Cover-Up

- The "cover-up" accusation assumes the pioneers invented the sanctuary doctrine
  after the disappointment
- But the sanctuary doctrine was not invented — it was _discovered_ by returning
  to Scripture
  - Revelation 11:19 — The Most Holy Place opened in heaven (the period after
    1844 in Revelation's sequence)
  - Hebrews 9:23 — Heavenly things need cleansing
  - Leviticus 16 — Day of Atonement pattern
- The pioneers did what honest Bible students do: kept the confirmed date,
  corrected the misunderstood event
  - [EB]: Acts 1:6–7 — The disciples had the right Person (Jesus) but the wrong
    event (political kingdom). Jesus corrected the event, not the faith.
  - [Aside]: The disciples on the Emmaus road had the right Messiah but the
    wrong expectation. Disappointment didn't mean they were wrong about
    everything — it meant they needed deeper study (Luke 24:25–27).
- [EGW]: "The scripture which above all others had been both the foundation and
  the central pillar of the advent faith was the declaration: 'Unto two thousand
  and three hundred days; then shall the sanctuary be cleansed.'" (GC 409.1)

## FINAL PEAK SECTION — The Sanctuary Reveals God's Plan (5 min)

### A. The Sanctuary Is the Gospel in Miniature

- Every element of the sanctuary points to Christ:
  - Altar of burnt offering — the cross
  - Laver — baptism and sanctification
  - Table of showbread — Christ the bread of life
  - Lampstand — Christ the light of the world
  - Altar of incense — Christ's intercession
  - Ark of the covenant — God's law, mercy seat, God's presence
  - [WB]: List the furniture → Christ connection
- Psalm 77:13 — "Thy way, O God, is in the sanctuary"
  - God's _way_ — His method of saving, restoring, judging — is revealed in the
    sanctuary
  - Remove the sanctuary, and you lose the roadmap of salvation

### B. We Live in the Day of Atonement

- The antitypical Day of Atonement has been underway since 1844
- This is the most solemn period in earth's history
  - Leviticus 23:29 — Those who did not afflict their souls were cut off
  - The heavenly reality is more serious than the earthly type
- [WB]: Write 'WE LIVE IN THE ANTITYPICAL DAY OF ATONEMENT — 1844 → NOW'
- [RQ]: If the Day of Atonement required Israel to search their hearts and
  afflict their souls, what does its heavenly antitype require of us? (Lev
  23:29)

### C. The Sanctuary Doctrine Is Not Optional

- It is the framework that holds together:
  - The law (in the ark)
  - The Sabbath (creation memorial, in the fourth commandment, in the ark)
  - Christ's priesthood (Hebrews)
  - The judgment (Day of Atonement)
  - The three angels' messages (Rev 14 — judgment hour)
- Remove the sanctuary and every distinctive unravels

### Call to Action — Enter the Most Holy Place by Faith

- **Believe** that Christ is ministering in the heavenly sanctuary _now_ — not
  seated in retirement but actively interceding for you (Heb 7:25; 8:1–2)
- **Understand** that the 2300-day prophecy points to 1844 with mathematical
  certainty — the same principle that predicted Christ's baptism predicts this
  date (Dan 8:14; 9:24–27)
- **Accept** that we live in the antitypical Day of Atonement — a time for
  solemn heart-searching and complete surrender (Lev 23:29)
- **Rejoice** that the sanctuary reveals God's full plan to save, cleanse, and
  restore you (Psa 77:13)
- **Resolve**: _I will study the sanctuary as the key to understanding God's
  work for me and in me_
