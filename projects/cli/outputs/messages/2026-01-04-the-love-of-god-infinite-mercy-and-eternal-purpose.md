- 1 John 4:8 — God's essence defined as love
- [WB]: Write "GOD = LOVE" at center (1 John 4:8)
- Not merely an attribute but the very nature of God
- [RQ]: If God is love, how should this shape our understanding of every doctrine? (1 John 4:8)
- All truth flows from and reveals this love

---

## Section 1 — The Nature of God's Love (7 min)

- Jeremiah 31:3 — "I have loved thee with an everlasting love"
  - Love without beginning or end
  - [WB]: Timeline arrow extending infinitely both directions — "EVERLASTING" (Jer 31:3)
- Jeremiah 31:3b — "With lovingkindness have I drawn thee"
  - Divine initiative, not human pursuit
  - [RQ]: Who makes the first move in salvation—God or man? (Jer 31:3)
- 1 John 4:10 — Herein is love: not that we loved God, but that He loved us
  - Love defined by action, not feeling
  - [WB]: Arrow from GOD → US, not US → GOD (1 John 4:10)
- [EGW]: "The love of God is something more than a mere negation; it is a positive and active principle, a living spring, ever flowing to bless others." (SC 77.2)
- [EB]: Deuteronomy 7:7–8 — God's love not based on human worth
- [Aside]: A drowning man does not rescue himself; the lifeguard dives in first.

---

## Section 2 — Love Demonstrated at the Cross (7 min) [*]

- John 3:16 — "God so loved the world, that he gave"
  - Love measured by sacrifice, not sentiment
  - [WB]: Write beneath LOVE → "GAVE His only begotten Son" (John 3:16)
- Romans 5:8 — "While we were yet sinners, Christ died for us"
  - Love given to enemies, not friends
  - [RQ]: What does it reveal about God that He loved us at our worst? (Rom 5:8)
- 1 John 4:9 — Love manifested that we might live through Him
  - Purpose: restoration of life lost in Eden
  - [WB]: Cross at center connecting GOD → LIFE → US (1 John 4:9)
- [EGW]: "The cross of Calvary reveals to the dullest understanding the love of God." (DA 652.1)
- [EB]: Isaiah 53:5 — Wounded for our transgressions
- [EB]: Galatians 2:20 — "Loved me, and gave himself for me"

---

## Section 3 — Love That Transforms and Prepares (6 min)

- 1 John 4:19 — "We love him, because he first loved us"
  - Love received becomes love returned
  - [WB]: Circle connecting GOD → US → GOD (1 John 4:19)
- Revelation 3:19 — "As many as I love, I rebuke and chasten"
  - Love includes correction and refinement
  - True love seeks eternal good, not temporary comfort
  - [RQ]: Why does genuine love include discipline? (Rev 3:19)
- 2 Corinthians 3:18 — Changed into the same image from glory to glory
  - Love transforms character
  - [WB]: Write — LOVED → TRANSFORMED → PREPARED (2 Cor 3:18)
- [Aside]: A loving parent disciplines precisely because they love; indifference ignores.
- [EGW]: "True love is not a strong, fiery, impetuous passion… It is calm and deep in its nature." (SC 53.3)
- [EB]: Hebrews 12:6 — Whom the Lord loveth He chasteneth

---

## FINAL PEAK SECTION — Will You Receive and Reflect This Love? (5 min)

- 1 John 4:16 — "We have known and believed the love that God hath to us"
  - Love must be known, believed, and dwelt in
  - [WB]: Underline KNOWN + BELIEVED + DWELL (1 John 4:16)
- 1 John 4:11 — "If God so loved us, we ought also to love one another"
  - Received love creates obligation
- Revelation 14:12 — Saints characterized by faith and obedience flowing from love
  - Love is the power behind end-time faithfulness
- [RQ]: If God's love cost Him everything, what response does such love demand from you? (1 John 4:11)
- [EGW]: "Love is the basis of godliness… The soul that is transformed by the grace of Christ will be filled with love." (COL 384.1)

### Call to Action — Eternal Decision

- **Believe** the love God has for you now (1 John 4:16)
- **Surrender** every resistance to that love (Rom 5:8)
- **Let His love transform** your character for eternity (2 Cor 3:18)
- **Commit to reflect** His love in obedient loyalty, prepared to stand in judgment (1 John 4:11; Rev 14:12)
